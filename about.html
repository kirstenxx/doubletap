About.

More About DoubleTap Gaming

Brainchild of three Indianapolis locals, DoubleTap Gaming was created to provide a platform for video game enthusiasts to get the most out of an exponentially growing community. Our goal was simple, we wanted to put the power and control back in your hands.

Our team wanted to build something that was user friendly, and didn't take all the profits as listing fees. We've had humble beginnings, and we wanted that DoubleTap to show that.

DoubleTap was built on industry standards, and we pride ourself on making sure everything works well for you. Do you have a recommendation on how we can make this better or did you find an issue? Email us, and we'll get it fixed as soon as possible.
